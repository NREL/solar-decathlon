Where Are the Solar Decathlon Houses Now?

Since 2002, 112 collegiate teams have participated in the U.S. Department of Energy Solar Decathlon. Their houses—now located throughout the United States and around the world—continue to serve numerous education-, conservation-, and community-oriented functions.

To learn more about where each house from Solar Decathlon 2002 through Solar Decathlon 2011 is today, choose from the links in the following tables or select a pin in the map below.
