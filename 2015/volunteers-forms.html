U.S. Department of Energy Solar Decathlon 2015 volunteers are required to turn in the following forms prior to their first shift:

  • Emergency Medical Release Form
  • Waiver of Liability Form
  • Parental Consent Form.

These forms are available on the volunteer registration website.

You can print out and complete the emergency medical release, waiver of liability, and (if you are under the age of 18) parental consent forms and arrive to your first shift with them. Alternatively, you may email completed forms in advance of your shift to volunteers@confluencec.com.
