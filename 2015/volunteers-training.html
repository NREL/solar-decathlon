All U.S. Department of Energy Solar Decathlon 2015 volunteers are required to complete a one-hour webinar-based training prior to working their first shift. Docents require additional study to prepare their tours and become comfortable speaking about the teams, the competition, and renewable energy. Training materials will be provided.
