Solar Decathlon

Acceptable on second and subsequent references only. Also Solar Decathlon 2013, if a year designation is needed. See U.S. Department of Energy Solar Decathlon.

Solar Decathlon 2013 contests

Solar Decathlon contest names are capitalized when used in their formal sense. The decathlon consists of 10 contests in which teams earn points based on task completion, monitored performance, and/or jury evaluation.

The 10 contests of the Solar Decathlon 2013 competition are:

  1. Architecture Contest
  2. Market Appeal Contest
  3. Engineering Contest
  4. Communications Contest
  5. Affordability Contest
  6. Comfort Zone Contest
  7. Hot Water Contest
  8. Appliances Contest
  9. Home Entertainment Contest
  10. Energy Balance Contest.

Also capitalize contest names in their numbered form, e.g. Contest 10. Numbered contest references should be used only in competition documents and team and organizer communications. Public communications should always use the full contest names.

Also see juried contest and measured contest.
