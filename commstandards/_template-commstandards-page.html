Solar Decathlon Style Guide

The U.S. Department of Energy Solar Decathlon Style Guide is an essential tool for preparing Solar Decathlon outreach materials, including Web products and printed publications. Consult this guide first when you develop or edit a Solar Decathlon communications product.

If you can't find an entry on a subject or topic in this guide, consult these style and reference guides in the following order:

  • The EERE Communication Standards and Guidelines Style Guide
  • The Associated Press Stylebook
  • Merriam-Webster's Collegiate Dictionary.

To ensure quality and consistency, all printed and electronic materials created for the U.S. Department of Energy Solar Decathlon should adhere to the following guidelines. Contact Carol Laurie, Solar Decathlon communications manager, by e-mail or by phone at 303-275-3655 for additional information.
