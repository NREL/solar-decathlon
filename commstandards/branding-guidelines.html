Solar Decathlon Identity Guidelines

U.S. Department of Energy Solar Decathlon organizers have developed identity guidelines to encourage consistency, quality, and accuracy in all Solar Decathlon communications products. Whether you are a Solar Decathlon team member, sponsor, or partner, these guidelines can help you develop communications and inspire actions that represent the event's ideals and character.

The documents presented below are designed to help you align your communications and outreach efforts with the U.S. Department of Energy Solar Decathlon brand.

Team Identity Guidelines

Solar Decathlon competition teams are encouraged to use the Team Identity Guidelines to direct their communications efforts.

Information about Solar Decathlon Communications Contest requirements for teams can be found in the Solar Decathlon rules.
