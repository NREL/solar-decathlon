Solar Decathlon Communication Standards

Teams, sponsors, partners, and contractors all create communications and outreach materials related to the U.S. Department of Energy Solar Decathlon. To help these groups in their efforts and ensure consistency and accuracy, the Solar Decathlon communications team has developed a collection of standards and tools to guide the development of Solar Decathlon-themed communications products.
