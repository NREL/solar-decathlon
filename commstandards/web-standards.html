Solar Decathlon Web Standards

All U.S. Department of Energy Solar Decathlon Web and electronic communication products must adhere to federal requirements and Solar Decathlon communication standards.

Website Standards

Solar Decathlon Website Standards

Solar Decathlon-branded Web and electronic communication products are governed by the Department of Energy Office of Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy Communication Standards and Guidelines. Peruse these standards and contact the Solar Decathlon Web coordinator before beginning a Web or electronic communication product.

Team Website Standards

Solar Decathlon team websites must meet the requirements outlined in the Solar Decathlon Rules.

Social Media Standards

Multiple social media accounts are maintained by the Solar Decathlon organizers as well as individual Solar Decathlon teams.

Promote Solar Decathlon on Social Media

Help us promote Solar Decathlon on your social media channels by:

  • Highlighting the competition’s accounts
    • When discussing Solar Decathlon online, link to the competition’s social media profiles.
    • Follow Solar Decathlon’s activity for regular content to share and promote on your official accounts.

  • Highlighting and promoting the teams
    • The Solar Decathlon teams are the stars of the competition and provide a plethora of visual content on their social media properties, ripe for promotion.
    • Almost all teams have and actively use both Facebook and Twitter as part of their social media strategies, and some have blogs, YouTube, Flickr, Vimeo, Pinterest, and Instagram accounts, among other properties.

Solar Decathlon Naming References

Follow these guidelines when referencing the Solar Decathlon:

  • Preferred
    • U.S. Department of Energy Solar Decathlon
    • DOE Solar Decathlon 2015
    • DOE Solar Decathlon
    • Solar Decathlon 2015

  • Not preferred, per Solar Decathlon branding guidelines
    • 2015 Solar Decathlon
    • U.S. Department of Energy’s Solar Decathlon 2015
